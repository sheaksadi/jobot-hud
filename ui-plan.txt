A bot is one algorithm instance / config running on one pair of coins.

For each "Bot":

General Control:
    Actions to start/stop
    State view
    config view/edit

Market Overview:
    CEX Price,
    DEX Prices

    Arbitrage Opportunities/Spread

    Liquidity Distribution

    Total COIN1
    Total COIN2

    CEX Holdings....
    DEX Holding....

Arbitrage View:
    Currently Placed orders (with visual representation relative to current prices)
    Indicator when order got executed, showing currently queued reverse trades, executed reverse trades...

    Past 5 trades with their respective reverse trades (summary with volume, spread, profit...)
    Link to full Log/History view where all past executions with their reverse trades can be explored.
